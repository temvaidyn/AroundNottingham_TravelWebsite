Places to Stay in Nottingham

Crowne Plaza Hotel Nottingham

Crowne Plaza Nottingham is one of the most stylish hotels in the Midlands. In the City Centre nearby many Government offices, both Universities, the Queens Medical Centre and Casinos. Crowne Plaza Nottingham has air conditioning in every bedroom plus an indoor swimming pool. Close to East Midlands Airport and the city centre attractions and shops, we have 210 bedrooms including flat screen TV's, mini bars and laptop safes (in most rooms).
In addition we have a choice of bar and dining options, a leisure club and our own 600-space car park with direct access into the hotel.

Jurys Inn Nottingham

Jurys Inn Nottingham is one of the best placed hotels in the city located less than 5 minutes' walk from the train station, tramway and car park. Whether visiting Nottingham for business or leisure, Jurys Inn Nottingham is the best place to stay allowing you easy access to Nottingham's many hidden gems. The Old Market Square, featuring a variety of shops, restaurants and bars, is a pleasant 15 minute walk away while Nottingham's Theatre Royal and Royal Concert Hall are only an 18 minute walk for those attending events there.
The hotel is also a short 10 minute jaunt to Nottingham's Capital FM Arena where major events and concerts take place. The hotel's 264 bedrooms have recently been refurbished and now feature stylish, modern decor and an ultra-comfy DREAM bed by Jurys Inn to unwind in.

View

Mercure Nottingham City Centre Hotel

Nottingham’s newest 4 star boutique hotel, now open following an 18 month, multi-million pound refurbishment. Originally a Georgian townhouse dating from 1822, the Mercure Nottingham City Centre has been restored to its former glory, reclaiming its place as Nottingham’s finest hotel.
Located in the heart of the fashionable Lace Market district, this chic property is perfectly placed only minutes from the Capital FM Arena, Old Market Square and Nottingham Castle.

View

Premier Inn Nottingham City Centre

Stay in the centre of town at our Nottingham Chapel Bar hotel - we're within walking distance of all Nottingham's top attractions, from the Capital FM Arena to the National Ice Centre and the Galleries of Justice Museum. Rock out at Rock City, watch Shakespeare come to life at the Nottingham Playhouse Theatre or see what's new from your favourite brands at intu Victoria Centre. You’re even close to the historic Lace Market, the magnificent Nottingham Castle and England’s oldest pub, Ye Old Trip to Jerusalem if you fancy a taste of some Nottingham history.
Once you've explored all the best bits of this friendly Midlands city, return to a delicious meal in our bar and grill and a great night's sleep in one of our extra comfy Hypnos beds.

Delta by Marriott Hotel Nottingham Belfry

The Nottingham Belfry is ideally located just one mile from the M1 and 5 miles from Nottingham city centre. The contemporary four-star hotel has 120 spacious bedrooms, a superb leisure club and tranquil spa.
The Nottingham Belfry is the region's largest conference venue as the hotel can hold up to 700 delegates in the Admiralty Suite or across 13 flexible conference and meeting rooms.

Travelodge Nottingham Riverside Hotel

If you’re looking for a family-friendly hotel in Nottingham, look no further than Travelodge Nottingham Riverside Hotel.
Nearby landmarks such as City of Caves (Tigguo Cobauc) (1.3 mi) and Old Market Square (1.4 mi) make Nottingham Travel Lodge a great place to stay when visiting Nottingham.
Guest rooms offer amenities such as a flat screen TV, and guests can go online with wifi offered by the hotel.
If you are driving to Nottingham Travel Lodge, free parking is available.

De Vere Orchard Hotel

Adjacent to the De Vere East Midlands Conference Centre, architects and interior designers have developed the eco-friendly building featuring accessible rooftop terraces, green roofs and maximum use of natural daylight. Spanning 330 acres, hotel incorporates an executive lounge, 160-cover restaurant, bar area, outside terrace, gym and four meeting rooms. Built to the highest environmental standards, the hotel will feature state-of-the-art technology to reduce carbon emissions. Solar photovoltaic panels and ground-sourced heat technology are also incorporated.

Lace Market Hotel Nottingham by Compass Hospitality

A stylish Georgian townhouse located in the heart Nottingham’s trendy Lace Market district. The Lace Market Hotel is the perfect place to stay whilst exploring the vibrant surrounding streets filled with historic buildings and plenty of shopping and entertainment venues. All the spacious and bright rooms at the Lace Market are individually designed and include flat-screen TVs, allergy-friendly bedding & minibars. Many of the rooms boast large floor-to-ceiling windows with spectacular views.

Hilton Nottingham

Explore the shopping and cultural delights of historic Nottingham from Hilton Nottingham hotel. This Nottingham hotel is a beautiful Victorian building situated in the heart of the city center, with contemporary décor and furnishings throughout the interior. Featuring a 60 foot indoor heated pool with Jacuzzi, the longest hotel pool in the city, a fully equipped LivingWell health club and fitness center and a business center for corporate events and business guests, this unique Nottingham hotel is a delight for guests visiting historic Nottingham on business or to take in the sights and sounds.
Step into Nottingham restaurant Nicholson’s Bar and Diner and enjoy modern and stylish American cuisine in an upscale diner setting, perfect for group dining or intimate romantic meals. Choose delicious cocktails from the extensive drinks menu and relax before a sampling the popular and lively night life that Nottingham has to offer.
